INVESTMENT PHILOSOPHY
Bottom-up fundamental analysis anchored in unit economics — not macro assumptions. Research begins with the question of how a business actually makes money, how durable that advantage is, and whether the current price reflects a misunderstanding of the answer.

Coverage gravitates toward quality businesses temporarily mispriced by narrative: companies where the market is asking the wrong question, and where the right question produces a differentiated view on terminal value.
