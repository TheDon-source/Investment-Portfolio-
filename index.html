Investment Philosophy
Bottom-up fundamental analysis anchored in unit economics rather than macro assumptions. Research begins with how a business actually makes money, how durable that competitive advantage is, and whether the current price reflects a misunderstanding of the answer. Coverage gravitates toward quality businesses temporarily mispriced by narrative, where the right question produces a differentiated view on long-run earnings power.
